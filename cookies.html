1. Introduction

This Cookie Policy explains how V-Runway uses cookies and similar technologies to recognize you when you visit our website. It explains what these technologies are and why we use them, as well as your rights to control our use of them.

2. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small data files placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used by website owners to make their websites work efficiently and provide reporting information.

2.1 Cookie Characteristics

  • Stored on your device
  • Enable website functionality
  • Improve user experience
  • Track website usage

3. Types of Cookies We Use

3.1 Essential Cookies

  • Authentication and security
  • Basic functionality
  • User session management
  • Load balancing

3.2 Analytics Cookies

  • Visitor behavior tracking
  • Performance monitoring
  • Error reporting
  • Usage patterns

3.3 Functionality Cookies

  • User preferences
  • Language settings
  • Region selection
  • Personalization

4. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Website operation and security
  • User experience improvement
  • Performance optimization
  • Analytics and statistics
  • Error tracking and debugging
  • Service personalization
  • Session management

5. Cookie Management

5.1 Browser Settings

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Accept or reject all cookies
  • Accept or reject specific cookies
  • Receive notifications for cookies
  • Delete existing cookies

5.2 Impact of Disabling Cookies

Disabling cookies may affect:

  • Website functionality
  • User experience
  • Performance optimization
  • Personalized features

6. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are placed by third-party services that appear on our pages:

  • Analytics providers
  • Performance monitoring
  • Security services
  • Feature integration

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
